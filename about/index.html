About

Since 2018, I've been collecting movie tickets as a way to capture memories and experiences across theaters near and far. What began with an average ticket price of just ¥10.0 has grown into a collection of 107 tickets, with an average ticket price of $41.03. In total, I've currently spent roughly $4389.90 on movie tickets. While most tickets are from theaters in Beijing, this collection also includes stubs from screenings in far-off places like Chiang Mai, Thailand, Malaysia, and Singapore. Each ticket represents a unique cinematic moment, spanning genres, cultures, and locations around the globe.
